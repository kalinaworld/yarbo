Meet Your Dealer

Frank Monteleone

Frank Monteleone has been building things in Sullivan County for over 35 years. Construction, site development, HVAC systems. He's the kind of person who figures out how things actually work and then makes them work better. He owns Monteleone Garden HydroFarm, one of several ventures he's built across the region.

When he came across Yarbo, he didn't just see a product to sell. He saw a piece of equipment that could genuinely help people up here, on the kinds of properties, slopes, and winters that most robotic mowers aren't built for. He tested it himself first, across local terrain and through real Catskills weather, before he ever offered it to anyone else.

If you buy through Frank, you're getting someone who has already put the machine through its paces. He'll tell you what it can handle, what it can't, and exactly how to set it up for your property.

Frank serves the Town of Highland in a leadership role and has been part of this community his whole life.
