About Us

Rooted in the Catskills

We're your neighbors — a locally owned business based in Sullivan County, New York, with deep roots in this community. When Yarbo's next-generation robotic mowers caught our attention, we knew they were exactly what Catskills homeowners needed: powerful enough for hilly, wooded terrain, smart enough to work unattended, and built to last.

We became an authorized Yarbo dealer because we believe in the product, and we back every sale with honest, hands-on support you can only get from someone who lives down the road.

Highland Yarbo is part of the Monteleone Gardens family — Frank Monteleone's garden center rooted right here in the Catskills. Stop in and see us.
